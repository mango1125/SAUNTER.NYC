A DWELLING FOR TWO SHADOWS

The Dwelling for Two Shadows, designed by Michael Myunghyun Lee, is a small artist's studio designed to both fulfill the basic requirements of an art studio, while also creating a space for the contemplation of material and shadow as light travels through the structure.

After we were approached to collaborate on presentation models for this project, we immediately sought ways to fully capture the ambiance and aesthetic qualities that the designer intended in our showcase. For this first demo, we chose a summer evening to demonstrate how we can use dramatic lighting in order to better convey the design's intention.

We are very excited to continue working with MEANINGLESS in order to both push our technology further and to help expedite their design and construction process.
