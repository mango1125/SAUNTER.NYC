ABOUT

Saunter is a design & technology studio. We create 3D models and tools for your business that can be shared in browser, on any platform, no problem.

Our team can create highly accurate, fast, and beautiful models of structures, places, products, graphics and more. Whether you want to change the game in client meetings, give your field engineers more accurate data, or overhaul your web presence, Saunter can help you get a head start for the transition into the 22nd century.

Our solutions are only limited by imagination. We welcome the opportunity to work with any industry partners that are interested in presenting data in interactive 3D. Please reach out at info@saunter.nyc.
